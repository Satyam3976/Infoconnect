About IIIT Una

Indian Institute of Information Technology Una (IIIT, Una) is one of the 20 new IIITs established in the country by MHRD under Public Private Partnership Mode. The IIITs are envisaged to be Institutes of National Importance. Aimed at generating highly competent manpower of global standards for the Industries, the institute acts as a major catalyst to develop new knowledge in the field of Information Technology. Currently the institute is functioning at its transit campus in NIT Hamirpur campus

Vision
